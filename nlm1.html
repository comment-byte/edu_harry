The Law of Inertia
Objects at rest stay at rest, and objects in motion stay in motion, unless acted upon by an external force.

üöÇ Practical Problem: Hogwarts Express
The Hogwarts Express (mass = 500,000 kg) pushes against the tracks with a force of 200,000 N to accelerate forward. What force do the tracks exert on the train, and in which direction?
Solution:
According to Newton's Third Law, every action has an equal and opposite reaction.
Action: Train pushes tracks backward with 200,000 N
Reaction: Tracks push train forward with 200,000 N
Answer: The tracks exert 200,000 N forward on the train

This reaction force is what propels the train forward!
